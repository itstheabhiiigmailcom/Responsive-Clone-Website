AIRCRAFT

Teal’s defense-focused drones offer a low-cost, portable solution designed for rapid deployment in any terrain. Built for field repairability and recoverability, they ensure mission continuity and minimal downtime. Equipped for Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR) missions, they provide real-time data and enhanced operational awareness.

CONTROLLERS

Teal controllers are specifically designed for seamless integration with Teal aircraft, ensuring an exceptional user experience when immediate and accurate visual intelligence is critical. To maximize operational efficiency, the controller’s batteries are swappable with the aircraft, providing continuous power and minimizing downtime in the field.
